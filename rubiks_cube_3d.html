📖 How to Use

🖱️ Mouse Controls

Click and drag to rotate the entire cube. Use the buttons to rotate individual faces.

🎯 Face Rotation

Click face rotation buttons (F, B, R, L, U, D) to turn that face clockwise.

🔄 Quick Actions

Scramble for a random cube, Solve to reset to solved state, Reset to clear all.
